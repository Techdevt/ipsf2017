GNAAS operates in eight zones which are:

  • Central Ghana Zone (Parts of Ashanti and Brong-Ahafo Regions)
  • Eastern Ghana Zone (The Eastern Region)
  • Midwest Zone (Parts of Central Region)
  • North Ghana Mission (Upper East, Upper West and Northern Regions)
  • South Ghana Zone One (Greater Accra and Volta Regions)
  • South Ghana Zone Two (Parts of Central Region)
  • South West Zone (Western Region)
  • South-Central Ghana Zone (Parts of Ashanti Region)

The National Secretariat rotates among institutions that meet all of the following criteria:

  • Minimum active membership of 250.
  • Commitment of members to both local and National activities.
  • A sound financial base.
  • The ability to host both Zonal and Inter-tertiary Retreat.

Currently, three GNAAS institutions meet these criteria and therefore the National Secretariat rotates among them. These institutions are:

  • GNAAS-University of Ghana.
  • GNAAS-Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology.
  • GNAAS-University of Cape-Coast.
